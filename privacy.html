In the current version, the app does not proactively collect, store, or share personally identifiable information (PII). Non‑personal data (e.g., device model, OS version, crash logs) may be generated by the operating system for troubleshooting only and is not used to identify individuals.

2. Third‑Party Services

If we integrate third‑party advertising or analytics services in future releases (e.g., Google AdMob, Apple, Tencent Youlianghui), we will update this policy to list each provider, the categories of data collected, purposes, retention, and opt‑out methods, and we will also provide an in‑app notice.

This app is intended for a general audience and is not designed specifically for children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor without parental consent, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

4. Data Security & Storage

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data security. Unless required by law, we do not use data for purposes unrelated to app functionality.
